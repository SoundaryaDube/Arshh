From sketch to glow

Every lamp is a small object of engineering.

We combine CAD modelling, additive manufacturing, and careful finishing so the final piece feels crafted, not mass-produced. The result is a lamp that looks sculptural in daylight and cinematic after sunset.

01

Design consultation

Share a mood, size, colour, name, room photo, or gifting brief. We translate it into a lamp direction.

02

3D model and preview

You get a digital concept with form, texture, and material choices before printing begins.

03

Print, finish, assemble

Layered parts are printed, cleaned, fitted with lighting hardware, and checked for final glow quality.
